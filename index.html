Grammar: Polite Requests

How do we ask for things politely at a restaurant?

"I want a Big Mac." (Sounds rude/childish)

"I would like a Big Mac." (Polite)

"Could I have a Big Mac?" (Very natural & native)

Which phrase should you avoid?

RULE UNLOCKED!

Avoid saying "I want" to service workers. Always use "Could I have..." or "I'd like...".

Practice with the teacher: Ask for a hamburger using "Could I have...?"
